## Process-to-process delivery

The Transport layer connects not just hosts but **the specific programs running on them**, using
**port numbers** to multiplex many conversations over one IP address. It offers two contrasting
contracts: **TCP** — connection-oriented, reliable, ordered, flow- and congestion-controlled — and
**UDP** — a thin, fast, connectionless datagram service. This is where the internet's reliability
actually lives, by design (the end-to-end principle).
