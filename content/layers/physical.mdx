## Moving bits as signals

The Physical layer is where data stops being an abstraction and becomes **physics**: voltages on
copper, pulses of light in fiber, modulated radio waves in the air. It defines everything needed to
put a raw stream of 1s and 0s onto a medium and recover it at the other end — connectors and pinouts,
signal levels, timing, data rates, and the **line coding** and **modulation** that map bits to
physical symbols.
